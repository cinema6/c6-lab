How to add a credit card

Guide for you to add a credit card

Step 1

Navigate to the Account Settings screen.

The dark gray bar on the far left side of the screen has the navigation tools that you need. Look for the button that reads "Account Settings," and click there to see your account information.

Step 2

Click "Manage Payment Options".

Click on the "Manage Payment Options" button below your payment information. This will take you to a screen where you can manage your payment options.

Step 3

Click "Credit Card."

Click the gray "Credit Card" button. This will open a modal where you can put in a new credit card.

Step 4

Enter the proper information.

Enter in your credit card's information. Making this a "Primary Card" means that all campaigns that you make will, by default, use this credit card first unless you choose otherwise when making a campaign.
